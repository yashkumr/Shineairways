About us

FOUNDER

Mr.Kailash Chand

Shine Airways is a travel company based in Noida, India, founded in 2021 by Mr. Kailash Chand. The company's mission is to provide affordable and hassle-free travel experiences to its customers, making travel accessible to everyone regardless of their budget or destination. In this blog post, we'll take a closer look at the story behind the company, the benefits it offers, and why it's a great choice for anyone looking for affordable travel with quality service. The idea for Shine Airways came to Mr. Chand when he was traveling with his father from Delhi to Mumbai.
The trip was not only expensive but also inconvenient, which made him realize that there was a need for a company that could provide affordable and hassle-free travel experiences to everyone. With this idea in mind, Mr. Chand decided to start his own company that would offer quality services at affordable prices.

OUR VISION

Shine Airways is committed to providing affordable travel options for our customers. Our team of experts curate travel packages that cater to all the needs and expectations of our customers. With an aim to expand our business nationwide, we will have certified travel agents at every Shine Airways location, who will offer comprehensive assistance to our users. Our ultimate goal is to make travel accessible and affordable for everyone.
